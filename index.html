The man behind the trust

A life lived for the village.

Shri Mahavir Singh was a freedom fighter, a lifelong social worker, and a political leader of Bihar. He believed independence meant nothing if the poorest farmer in the last mud-walled village still went without dignity.

"Aazaadi ka asli matlab hai — ek bachcha jo apni kahaani khud likh sake."

— Shri Mahavir Singh, 1979

On 28 November 1992, a year after his passing, his family and fellow workers registered this trust to carry his vision forward. Three decades later, that work reaches eight Rural Knowledge Centers across Madhubani.
