Planning a visit?आने का मन है?

How to get to Bishanpur.बिशनपुर कैसे पहुँचें।

We host visitors year-round — but October to March is the kindest weather. Write to us a week ahead and we'll arrange a pickup from the nearest station.हम साल भर अतिथियों का स्वागत करते हैं — पर अक्टूबर से मार्च मौसम सबसे सुखद होता है। एक सप्ताह पहले लिखिए, हम निकटतम स्टेशन से लाने की व्यवस्था कर देंगे।

✈

By airहवाई जहाज़ से

Darbhanga Airport (DBR) is the closest — roughly 70 km. Patna (PAT) is 4 hours by road if Darbhanga has no convenient flight.दरभंगा हवाई-अड्डा (DBR) निकटतम है — लगभग 70 कि.मी. यदि दरभंगा की कोई सुविधाजनक उड़ान न हो, तो पटना (PAT) से सड़क द्वारा 4 घंटे।

🚆

By train

Ghoghardiha (GGA) is a 10-minute drive from the office. Madhubani (MBI) is also well-connected from Delhi, Kolkata and Patna.

🛣

By road

From Patna: ~6 hours via NH-22 & NH-527E. Drivers familiar with rural Madhubani are easy to hire in Darbhanga or Madhubani town.

Frequently asked

Common questions, briefly answered.

Is my donation tax-deductible? +

Yes. We are registered under section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You'll receive an 80G receipt by email within 48 hours of any donation.

Can I donate from outside India? +

Our FCRA application is in process. Until it clears, international supporters often donate through partner foundations — write to us and we'll connect you with the right route.

How much of my donation reaches the field? +

Above 96%. We have no paid executives — trustees are volunteers. Administrative overhead covers audit, statutory filings and bank charges.

Can I visit a program before donating? +

Absolutely — please do. Write to us with a date and we'll arrange a half-day or full-day visit, plus a pickup from Ghoghardiha or Madhubani station.

How do I report a concern or grievance? +

Email the Chairperson directly at chairperson@mahavirtrust.org. All concerns are acknowledged within 7 days and resolved within 30.

Mahavir Singh Memorial Trustमहावीर सिंह स्मारक न्यास Est. 1992स्था. 1992

A public charitable Trust founded in 1992 by Dr. Binod Kumar Singh, with the support of prominent citizens of the region, in memory of freedom fighter Mahavir Singh (1907–1992). Supporting education and opportunity across Madhubani and Darbhanga districts of Bihar.1992 में डॉ. विनोद कुमार सिंह द्वारा, क्षेत्र के प्रबुद्ध नागरिकों के सहयोग से, स्वतंत्रता सेनानी महावीर सिंह (1907–1992) की स्मृति में स्थापित एक सार्वजनिक चैरिटेबल ट्रस्ट। बिहार के मधुबनी व दरभंगा ज़िलों में शिक्षा और अवसर के लिए कार्यरत।
