Give a gift. Sponsor a student. Spend a week in Bishanpur.

Beyond donations and volunteering.

In-kind giving

Books, computers, sewing machines, medical supplies — write to us before couriering. We'll tell you what's needed and where to send it.

We've delivered CSR projects with mid-sized Indian companies under section 135. Ask for our partnership brief.

Legacy giving
