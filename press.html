Surinamese-born scholar Chan Choenni visits a memorial plaque on the memorial wall of the Mahavir Singh Memorial Trust in Bishanpur — where the Global Girmitiya Lineage Archive Searching Center, established in March 2025, helps families around the world trace their indentured ancestors. Trust opens centre to preserve Girmitiya diaspora history

In response to PM Modi's call to compile a database of Girmitiya descendants, the Mahavir Singh Memorial Trust inaugurates the Global Girmitiya Lineage Archive & Searching Centre (GGLASC) at Bishnupur. Managing trustee Vinod Kumar Singh — a former IRS officer — describes the centre's mission to preserve, research and share the history of the Girmitiya diaspora. By Chandra Shekhar Jha Azad.

Mahavir Singh Memorial Trustमहावीर सिंह स्मारक न्यास Est. 1992स्था. 1992

A public charitable trust founded in 1992 by Dr. Binod Kumar Singh, with the support of prominent citizens of the region, in memory of freedom fighter Mahavir Singh (1907–1992). Supporting education and opportunity across Madhubani and Darbhanga districts of Bihar.1992 में डॉ. विनोद कुमार सिंह द्वारा, क्षेत्र के प्रबुद्ध नागरिकों के सहयोग से, स्वतंत्रता सेनानी महावीर सिंह (1907–1992) की स्मृति में स्थापित एक सार्वजनिक चैरिटेबल ट्रस्ट। बिहार के मधुबनी व दरभंगा ज़िलों में शिक्षा और अवसर के लिए कार्यरत।
